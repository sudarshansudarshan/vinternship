In this vlog, I share my learning journey of understanding Dependency Injection and Promises in TypeScript during my Vinternship.

I explain how Dependency Injection helps in writing loosely coupled, scalable, and testable code by separating concerns and managing object creation effectively. I demonstrate how I implemented it using TypeDI and how it improved the structure of my backend system.

I also walk through Promises and asynchronous programming, showing how async operations work behind the scenes, how `.then()` differs from `async/await`, and how I handled real debugging situations while building API endpoints.

This video reflects my real development process — what confused me initially, how I approached the problems, and how clarity came through hands-on implementation.
