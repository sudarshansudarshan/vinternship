##CHALLENGES I FACED

At the beginning, I faced many errors—server errors, logical errors, and sometimes things didn’t work at all.

Instead of getting discouraged, I learned how to read error messages carefully, search for solutions, and debug step by step.

Debugging was difficult at first, but slowly it became a skill, not something to fear.

## HOW MY THINKING CHANGED

Earlier, I used to focus only on making the code work.

But during this Vinternship, I learned the importance of writing clean, structured, and readable code.

## ADVICE TO BEGINNERS

If you are just starting your learning journey, my advice is: don’t rush, don’t compare yourself with others, and don’t be afraid of making mistakes.

Learning takes time, and struggling is part of the process.

Keep practicing, keep building small projects, and try to understand concepts instead of memorizing code.
