In this video, I share my internship journey through NPTEL at the Indian Institute of Technology Ropar, which turned into one of the most enriching learning experiences of my academic journey.

This internship was structured into multiple phases, including learning the MERN stack through the AI-powered platform ViBe, solving real-world case studies, participating in collaborative stand-up sessions, breakout discussions, and finally progressing toward the viva and endorsement phase.

✨ **Highlights of my journey:**

- MERN Stack learning with AI-proctored sessions  
- Interactive case studies and real-world problem solving  
- Stand-up meetings and peer collaboration  
- Breakout room discussions & community learning  
- Receiving the Golden Ticket among 300+ interns in the Euclideans cohort  
- Growth beyond technical skills — discipline, teamwork, and confidence
