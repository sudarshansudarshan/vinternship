##  My Vinternship Experience

In this vlog, I share my internship experience conducted under the guidance of IIT Ropar and Professor Sudarshan Iyengar, in collaboration with VLED Labs and the supporting team.

I discuss how I started as a complete beginner in the MERN stack and gradually understood how MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js work together to build full-stack applications. The internship helped me gain exposure to real-world development workflows, including structured project organization, debugging practices, and collaborative development environments.

I also talk about the stand-up sessions, breakout room discussions, and peer interactions that enhanced my communication, networking, and professional skills. This experience helped bridge the gap between academic learning and industry expectations.

Through this vlog, viewers will understand:
- How beginners can approach MERN stack development  
- The importance of practical exposure in internships  
- How collaborative learning improves technical growth
