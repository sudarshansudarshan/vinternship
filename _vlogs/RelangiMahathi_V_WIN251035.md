This vlog shares my Vinternship journey with VLED Lab, where I learned and explored the MERN stack. I talk about how my journey started through the NPTEL Python certification and how it led to my first internship experience.

In this video, I explain my learning process through the VIBE platform, the motivation I gained from leaderboard competition, and how case studies helped me improve my coding skills. I also share the challenges I faced, including managing academics and overcoming hesitation in speaking during stand-up sessions.

Additionally, I demonstrate one of my projects — a Smart Campus AI-based timetable and room management system — where I applied the concepts I learned.


Overall, this vlog reflects my growth in both technical skills and confidence throughout the internship.
