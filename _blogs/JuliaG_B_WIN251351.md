When I first joined the Vinternship under NPTEL IIT Ropar, I honestly did not know what to expect. I thought it would be like any other online course — watch videos, complete tasks, and move on. But this journey was very different.

The MERN stack learning through ViBe was not just about watching tutorials. It required consistency. At first, I felt overwhelmed. There were concepts I did not fully understand, and sometimes I doubted whether I was capable of completing everything.

The real challenge began with the case studies. They were not direct questions with direct answers. They required thinking. Sometimes I would stare at the screen not knowing how to begin. That feeling of confusion slowly taught me something important — growth does not come from comfort.

The weekly standup sessions were another new experience for me. Speaking about my progress made me more responsible. It pushed me to stay consistent because I knew I had to report what I had learned. The Saturday and Sunday live sessions gave clarity whenever I felt stuck. Listening to others share their doubts made me realize I was not alone in this journey.

What changed the most in me was not just technical knowledge. It was my mindset. I started approaching problems differently. Instead of asking, “Can I do this?”, I began asking, “How can I solve this?”

This Vinternship taught me discipline, consistency, and the courage to struggle. It showed me that learning is not about speed — it is about persistence.

Looking back, I may not know everything yet, but I now know how to learn. And that, I believe, is the most valuable lesson of this journey.
