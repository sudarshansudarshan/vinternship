I'm happy to share my experience so far in the 10-week virtual internship under NPTEL at IIT Ropar. The learning process has been engaging and well-structured, making it a valuable experience.

This quiz-based approach turned out to be one of my favorite aspects. It feels less like an assignment and more like a fun, interactive way to check how attentive we were during the sessions.
