When I first received this opportunity through NPTEL Star, I felt truly
fortunate. However, after missing the inaugural session, I experienced
self-doubt and felt overwhelmed in the beginning.

Instead of stepping back, I chose to seek guidance. My cohort mate,
Maaji Pranathi, kindly encouraged me to move forward with confidence.
Later, my interaction with E. Pranathi through LinkedIn helped clarify
my doubts. Those simple yet meaningful conversations strengthened my
confidence.

Being placed in breakout rooms with my previous cohort mates (Founders)
was also inspiring. Observing their confidence and happiness after
completing their internship motivated me to believe that I could achieve
it too.

The VIBE course was challenging initially. I could focus only for 1--2
hours, and network issues sometimes added to the frustration. But I
stayed consistent. Gradually, I improved, increased my focus to 2--3
hours, and completed the course successfully with 100%.

Now, I have started working on case studies and am slowly learning while
enjoying the concepts.

## My Motto

> **Don't give up. Do your best. Even if you fail, you gain
> experience.**

I also recognize the importance of being more active in live sessions
and peer discussions, and I am committed to improving in this area.

My sincere thanks to Rohit Sir, Suruchi Ma'am, and Nitin Sir for their
encouragement, and special appreciation to Rohit Sir and the team member
who supported me through screen sharing beyond stand-up time. I truly
admire the dedication of the team, guided by the inspiring leadership of
Sudharsan Sir.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

This journey has taught me that growth begins where comfort ends.
Consistency truly makes the difference.
