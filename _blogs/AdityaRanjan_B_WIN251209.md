## From Rankings to Real Growth – My Vinternship Experience  

When I got selected for the Pinternship, I felt confident. I had practiced DSA, explored machine learning basics, and built backend projects. I believed this internship would mainly help me apply what I already knew.  

Very soon, I realized it was going to push me beyond that comfort zone.  

In January, I became a top performer, and I completed ViBe in 4th position. That achievement meant a lot to me. It reflected consistency and effort. But as the weeks passed, I understood that ranks were only one part of the journey. The real transformation was happening in how I think and work.  

The standup meetings played a big role in that change. Explaining what I did, what blocked me, and what I planned next forced me to be clear and honest. I became more disciplined because I could not hide behind vague progress. It improved both my communication and my planning skills.  

Learning through ViBe was different from regular classes. It required self-control. No one forced me to complete modules. I had to pause, revise, experiment, and truly understand concepts. Finishing in 4th position was satisfying, but what mattered more was building the habit of consistent learning.  

The case studies were the most enjoyable part for me. They were not step-by-step guides. They required decision-making and patience. Many times, solutions did not work on the first attempt. Debugging stopped feeling like failure and started feeling like part of the process. I genuinely enjoyed solving them, and I will continue working on more.  

I just completed my midsems yesterday, and while preparing for exams, I noticed something important. My approach to problems has changed. I now break problems into smaller parts and think calmly instead of rushing toward answers.  

This Pinternship did not just improve my technical skills. It strengthened my resilience. I now measure growth by consistency, clarity, and the ability to handle confusion without panic.  

I am grateful to Prof. Sudarshan Iyengar and his team — Meenakshi V ma’am, Nitin Sankar sir, Jinal Gupta ma’am — for their guidance and support.  

Thankful to ANNAM.AI, Vicharanashala (Lab for Education Design), NPTEL, and IIT Ropar for this opportunity.  

This journey is still ongoing, and I am excited for what comes next.
