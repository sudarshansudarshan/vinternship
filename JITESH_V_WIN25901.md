## My Vinternship Experience

In this vlog, I share my journey from having zero knowledge about the MERN stack to building a basic foundation through the Vinternship program.

I talk about:
- How I started without confidence but with curiosity.
- My experience learning through the ViBe platform.
- The impact of stand-up sessions and live mentor interactions.
- How case studies were structured with analogies, code explanations, and hands-on practice.
- How deadlines and the progress dashboard kept me consistent and motivated.
- How this internship helped me grow both technically and personally.

This journey was not just about learning MERN — it was about building confidence, discipline, and the courage to start something new.
